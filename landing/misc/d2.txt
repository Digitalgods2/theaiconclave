## FLOW

- One answer is not enough.
- Builders need inspectable reasoning.
- Switchboard turns models into councils.
- It deliberates; humans decide.
- Dissent is preserved, not averaged.
- Three modes match decision urgency.
- Diverse seats expose different blind spots.
- Local orchestration keeps records under control.
- Every task becomes auditable memory.
- Decisions feed future deliberations.
- Coding follows a clean four-step loop.
- Execution stays outside the council.
- Sandboxes provide safe project context.
- Images require honest perceptual disagreement.
- Operability governs every new capability.
- Start small, then record real decisions.
- Accountable AI means human-kept judgment.

## DESIRED TAKEAWAY

AI Switchboard makes AI useful for serious work by turning model answers into governed, auditable deliberations where humans retain authority and decisions become reusable memory.
