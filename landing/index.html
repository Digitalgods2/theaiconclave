What it does

Everything you need.
Nothing you don't.

Built for builders, writers, researchers, and technical creators who need more than a chatbot — they need governed deliberation with an audit trail.

⚖️

Three Deliberation Modes

Conclave — equal voices, full-mesh visibility. Consult — one primary, the rest as critics. Resolve — one agent drives, others advise. Pick the shape that fits your decision.

🔍

Preserved Dissent

Disagreements are first-class records in the Conclave. The minority view is logged, exported, and permanently visible — not silently smoothed over by a false consensus.

👤

Human Authority

The council deliberates. You ratify. Your decision is recorded as the authoritative final word, separate from the AI output. AI advises. You decide. Always.

📋

Full Audit Trail

Every exchange is logged to a local SQLite database. Export any task as PDF, Word, Markdown, or plain text. Decision records are tier-protected — they're never auto-deleted.

🏠

Local-First Orchestration

Runs entirely on 127.0.0.1. Your deliberations never pass through a Conclave server. API calls go directly to each provider — no middleman, no data escrow.

📁

Project Sandbox

Attach your codebase. Agents receive a read-only snapshot — never the live files. They reason on your actual code, not a description of it. Your project is never modified.

🔗

Thread Continuity

A resolved task seeds the next one. The council carries forward your decision and the final answer as context. You build on what you've established — no re-explaining your starting point.

💰

Spend Transparency

Live model pricing, daily spend charts, and per-agent token counts — built into the dashboard. Mix subscription CLIs with pay-per-token OpenRouter seats. A typical conclave turn costs $0.001–$0.02.

How it works

From question to ratified decision
in three steps.

1

Submit your question

Choose your agents, your deliberation mode, and optionally attach your codebase or a URL. Permissions are explicit — agents only do what you explicitly allow, per task.

2

Watch the council deliberate

Agents exchange positions round by round. You watch it happen in real time on the dashboard — every turn, every disagreement, every shift in reasoning is visible as it happens.

3

Ratify the outcome

Record your decision in your own words — what you chose, why, what you rejected. The record is stored permanently alongside the full transcript and exports as PDF, Word, or Markdown.

The Conclave Charter

Governed by design.

The AI Conclave runs under a ratified charter — binding rules embedded into every agent prompt at runtime. Not a setting. Not a toggle. Structural.

Read-only by default

Agents cannot write files, run commands, or install packages unless you explicitly grant each permission — per task, never globally. Default-deny, not default-allow.

No in-conclave execution

Agents reason about your code. They don't run it. The deliberation is always about a stable, read-only snapshot. No surprises, no side-effects inside a session.

Operability before capability

New features only land when the operational foundation is solid. No half-built capabilities, no silent failure modes. The charter is enforced in code, not in promises.

Append-only decision records

Ratified decisions are never deleted or edited — only superseded, with the original record preserved as audit history. The past is permanent. That's the point.
